  • You stumble upon a strange smartphone on your 25th birthday. You do not know anything about its manufacturer and the device does not respond to your actions. However, you feel a strange desire to keep the phone, so you put it in your pocket just in case.
  • On your 45th birthday, you feel your phone trembling uncontrollably in your pocket. You take it out and the constant trembling suddenly stops. To your strange surprise, you find the inscription "Loading" in the center of the screen.
  • On your 65th birthday, you are again faced with a strange, inexplicable shake from your strange smartphone. But this time, a window with a green button appears on the screen. Although you don't understand the language of this window and the button, you realize that you still don't have tried everything, so you decide to press the button to see what happens.
    By pressing the green button, you will be reborn and you will have to start life anew, having lost all your levels and money. However, you will receive experience multipliers for your positions and abilities, equivalent to: (1 + max position or ability level / 10)1.001. This means that you will learn everything again much faster than in a past life.
    Something suggests that the window on the screen may change if you wait another about two hundred years...

  • Your gut instinct was correct. The moment you turn 200, you hear an ominous buzz coming from your phone. The image on the screen shakes and distorts, and then you hear a whisper: "So, you've come this far ... You achieve significant power, I see ... Are you ready to go further?". After that, a yellow button appears on the screen.
    If you decide to push the yellow button, all of your levels, money and even max levels will be reset. You will be reborn as a fresh slate. However, you will unlock a new possibilities and gain corruption, which will heavily impact your future lives.

  • A thousand years have come and gone and yet, you still remain alive... somehow. You feel your amulet shivering again and to your surprise, you see small tendrils full of eyeballs emergin from it. As you are about to drop the amulet, you hear frightening screech followed by whispers in your head... "You have to embrace the Void..."
    You have unlocked "The Void" class category along with "Void Manipulation" abilities.

  • Ten millenia have passed... most of them spent serving the Void, yet there is still a glimpse of humanity inside of you. All of the sudden your amulet starts to glow in the distance, illuminating darkness around you with the brightness of thousand suns. With the very last remnant of your free will you run to your amulet and touch it... "You are now part of something bigger than yourself..."
    You have unlocked "Galactic Council" class category along with "Celestial Powers" abilities.
    By clicking on Transcend, you will be reborn once again, losing all your coins, corruption and current levels. However your max level won't be erased but multiplied with Cosmic Recollection ability and you will gain Inner Experience, new ability that will boost you even further in your journey.
    Transcend will only show up if you have Cosmic Recollection ability unlocked!
